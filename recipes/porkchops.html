Yummy Pork Chops

Make this super easy and suprisingly delicious pork chops with easy to find ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 boneless pork chops

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the Italian-style salad dressing, soy sauce, and black pepper
  2. Place the pork chops in a skillet over medium heat, and cover with the dressing mixture. Cover skillet and cook pork chops 25 minutes, turning ocassionally. Remove cover, reduce heat to low, and continue cooking to desired doneness.
